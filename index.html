Biography

I am currently pursuing an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree in Medical Imaging and Applications (MAIA), coordinated by the University of Girona, Spain, along with the Université de Bourgogne, France, and the University of Cassino, Italy as partner universities. Also, I am a research collaborator with Prof. Yongjian Hu's Lab at the South China University of Technology, and Prof. Yu Guan at the University of Warwick, UK.

I have contributed to various projects funded by Pakistan's National ICT Research, and United Nations Development Program, as a research assistant and data science coordinator. My contribution related to the Caesarean Section Surgery was part of the National Healthcare Innovation Plan to reduce the infant mortality rate of Pakistan.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore, Pakistan. My thesis, "Sentiment Analysis of the United States of America Airlines based on NLP" was supervised by Dr. Amna Yasin. Also, I got the chance to do a Summer of Code internship at JSK Lab, University of Tokyo, Japan, working on the prediction of robotics execution failures using deep learning models, with funding from Google.

Research Interests

I have broad interests in medical imaging analysis, deep learning, and natural language processing. My current research topics are about:

  • Medical Image Analysis
    - diabetic retinopathy severity classification/lesions segmentation
    - edge detection/segmentation of brain tumor MRI images

  • Learning with Low Supervision
    - weakly supervised image segmentation
    - semi-supervised/unsupervised cross-domain image classification

  • Natural Language Processing
    - sentiment analysis/emotion AI
    - captions generation from images
